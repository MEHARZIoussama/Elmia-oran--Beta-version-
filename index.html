About Elmia

Built on three pillars

Elmia Oran combines a demanding academic program with genuine care for every student's growth — in mind, values, and confidence.

Academic Excellence

A rigorous curriculum in sciences, languages and mathematics, taught by qualified, experienced teachers.

Character Building

Daily values of respect, discipline and responsibility woven into school life, not just a subject on a timetable.

Innovation & Technology

Smart classrooms, science labs and coding workshops that prepare students for tomorrow, not just the exam.
